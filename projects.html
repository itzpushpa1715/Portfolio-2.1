Single Acting Cylinder

A single-acting cylinder is a type of hydraulic or pneumatic actuator that uses pressure to move in one direction while relying on an external force, such as a spring or gravity, for the return stroke. These cylinders are widely used in applications requiring linear motion, including clamping, lifting, and pressing tasks. Their simple design ensures efficiency, reliability, and ease of maintenance.

Single Acting Cylinder with Relay

A Single Acting Cylinder with Relay uses relay logic to control the cylinder's motion, enabling automated linear actuation. It integrates switches, valves, and relays for precise, efficient operation.This report focuses on a single-acting cylinder setup controlled by a relay system. It includes a detailed explanation of its functionality, the devices utilized in the setup, and their purposes. The schematic diagram from the provided material is analyzed to describe the control and operational logic.
